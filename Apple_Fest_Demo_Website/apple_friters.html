Apple Fritters

The Monks on the Commons is actually not only a vendor at the Apple Fest, but it is also a delicious resturaunt just by itself. However, we are here to talk about Apple Fest, and the Monks is the only place that has Apple Fritters, and they will leave you speechless. The Apple Fritters are nice and crunchy on the outside but tenderly soft on the inside. The addition of some powdered sugar to top it off, makes a masterpiece.

$5 per apple fritter serving

$4 for hot apple cider

$6 for spiked cider
