Caramel Apples

Prices Caramel Apples

It doesn't get much simpler than this, maybe other than just a regular apple, but nonetheless in sweets relating to apples, caramel apples have been a classic since who knows when. The caramel apples you get here are simply fantastic. The apples are nice, cold, and juicy while the caramel, which they stir right in front of you, is heart-meltingly warm. The combination is unbeatable, and to top it off, you get a toppings choice of chocolate fudge or whipcream, and there is no line!
