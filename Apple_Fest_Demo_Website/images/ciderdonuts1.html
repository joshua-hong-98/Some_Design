Apple Cider Donuts

Prices for Cider Donuts

Apple Cider Donuts are a very traditional pastery at the Apple Fest. It has been available every Apple Fest and there are always big lines for the donuts. Although this year there is competition for the vendor, these Cider Donuts have garnered long lines for a reason. The donuts though not so sweet, they are amazingly soft and is a surprising treat for those who have a more mild taste for sweets.

$1 for 1 donut

$5 for 2 donuts and choice of hot or cold apple cider
