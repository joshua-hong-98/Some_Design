Apple Cider Donuts

Prices for Cider Donuts

Although Apple Cider Donuts have been the staple of the Apple Fest, there has always been only one vendor selling these delicious pastery. However, this year, for the first time there were two vendors selling Cider Donuts, and this vendor happened to be one of them. Run by a local Amish group, these donuts differed from the other Cider Donuts as they were covered in smooth, sugary glaze. The donuts are fried right in front of you, which takes longer, but it is definitely worth the wait for these donuts as they are blissfully sweet.

$5 for half a dozen

$10 for a dozen
